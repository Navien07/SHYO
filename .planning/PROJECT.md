# Seputeh HYO Website

## What This Is

An interactive, trilingual (English, Malay, Tamil) website for Seputeh Hindu Youth Organisation (Seputeh HYO), a Hindu youth NGO based in the Seputeh parliamentary area, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. The site serves as the organisation's digital home — showcasing their mission, programmes, and team, while enabling youth to join as members and the public to access documentation and contact the organisation.

## Core Value

Youth and community members can discover Seputeh HYO, understand their work, and take action — whether joining as a member, exploring programmes, or collaborating — all in their preferred language.

### Out of Scope

- Member login / gated content — documentation is fully public, no authentication for visitors
- E-commerce or donation payment gateway — not part of v1
- Real-time chat or forum — contact via form/WhatsApp is sufficient for v1
- Mobile app — web-first approach

## Context

- Organisation established: 16 September 2002
- Target audience: Malaysian youth (15–40), community members, potential collaborators, sponsors
- Focus areas: Youth leadership, cultural preservation, community service, social awareness, well-being
- Existing branding: Logo and colour palette already defined — must be incorporated
- Contact channels: Contact form, email address, WhatsApp link, social media (Facebook, Instagram, etc.)
- Content language: Trilingual — English, Bahasa Malaysia, Tamil

## Constraints

- **CMS**: Non-technical admin must be able to upload PDFs and update programmes without developer help
- **Language**: All user-facing pages must support EN / BM / Tamil toggle
- **Branding**: Must use existing Seputeh HYO logo and colours — no rebranding
- **Accessibility**: Public-facing site, must be readable and navigable on mobile (many Malaysian youth browse on phones)
